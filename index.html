I was bored one day and wanted to explore making an ant hill simulation.
I came up with this in about 4 hours.

There are plenty of bugs, but for now I'm happy with it
The end goal was to have something interesting to watch as the ants built a colony

What I would add if I had more time to mess with it:
    Tunnels that loop back to eachother (right now it's just a tree)
    Support tunnels making it back to surface (right now there's just one entry point and no tunnels can go back up certain elevation)
    Add rooms that would have to be built after so much distance from the nearest room/surface point     Support more than the 8 secondary directions
